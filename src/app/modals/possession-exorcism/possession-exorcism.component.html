To exorsise a spirit that is possessing, others must defeat it in spirit combat and choose to exorcise it.

A possessing spirit or soul can willingly abandon a possession at any time.

Dominant Possession

Spirit takes complete control of the victim. The victim is aware but powerless to stop it.

The spirit has no access to the victim's memories, skills or magic, but it substitutes its own instead.

To actively detect a dominant possession requires a successful, unopposed test of either Insight if viewed magically, or Trance.

Passive Possession

Passive Possession occurs when the spirit lodges itself withing the victim, but is unable to seize control, allowing the host to remain in full command of its faculties

Instead the spirit 'merely' inflicts its powers on the host's body.

Convert Possession

Just like Passive Possession but the spirit remains hidden. The spirit might take over the body at night while the victim is asleep to keep its existence secret.

Detecting a covert possession requires a successful Opposed roll of either Insight or Trance versus the spirit's Stealth skill.
