Placement of weapons and shields so that they act as cover.

  • Characters hold weapon/shield so it covers a chosen Hit Location (or Hit Locations when using a shield). They cannot then actively Parry with it (see Ward Location page 93).
  • Any Attack which hits those Hit Locations reduces damage as normal
  • If Defender has two weapons or weapon and shield combatant can use other weapon to actively Parry
  • Choose Location Special Effect cannot be used to bypass the block
  • Crouching down behind a shield allows a character to double the number of Hit Locations covered whilst Passive Blocking.
