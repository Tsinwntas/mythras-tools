Attacker

  • At least one full Combat Round of movement at running or sprinting speed prior to contact
  • Attack roll minus one Difficulty Grade
  • +1 step to bipedal Attacker's Damage Bonus
  • +2 steps to the the Damage Bonus of four (or more) legged creatures by two steps
  • +1 step to the size of the attacking weapon

Defender

  • Parrying, risk of knockback if damage > SIZ
  • Evade, if the attacker has a higher level of success then calculate special effects as normal, otherwise, defender moves out of the way. In either case, defender ends up prone.
  • Counterattacking,
    • Both sides are treated as having failed to Parry, although they can still benefit from Passive Blocking.
    • Longest Reach strikes first
    • Longshafted impaling weapons which can be 'set' against a Charge, the Defender may use opponent's or its mount' Damage Bonus
