Luck points can be used in a variety of ways. Only one Luck Point can be used in support of a particular action.

Cheat Fate

A luck point can be used to force a re-roll on any kind of dice roll.

Desperate Effort

Can spend a luck point to gain an additional Action Point if that character run out of Action Points.

Mitigate Damage

The moment a character suffers a Major Wound, that character can spend a luck point to downgrade the injury to a Serious Wound. This reduces the damage taken to one Hit Point less than what would be required to inflict a Major Wound.

Using a Luck Point, to re-roll a particular result for example, is a Free action.
