• Avoid missiles - vs Combat style of attacker
  • Thwarting a trap - vs either by Potency of Trap or skill rating of maker at the time of making the trap
  • Evading a spell - vs spell caster's score in the casting skill
  • Manoeuvering in combat - vs attacker's evade skill
  • Whatever the result evader ends up prone
  • A character can keep evading while prone, but with the appropriate penalties
  • Mounted character can only evade by flinging themselves from the saddle
