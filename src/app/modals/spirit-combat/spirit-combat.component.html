In the Spirit world, Spirit combat doesn't have to look like actual combat and can take any form the GM deems appropriate.

If visible in the Mortal World, a character with Magic can intervene.

Mortal combatants in the spirit world use their Spirit Attributes.

Successful Spirit Attacks deal damage equal to the attacker's Spirit Damage roll to the defender's Magic Points.

Combat continues until the fight is concluded by use of a Special Effect, or one of the combatants is reduced to zero Magic Points, at which point they are defeated.

Consequences of Defeat

  • Possessed: Some spirits can poses the loser's body
  • Bound: Loser spirit may be bound into a fetish, location or creature
  • Exorcised: A spirit possessing a mortal can be exorcised back to the Spirit World
  • Dissipated: If a spirit has the Deadly Ability, it can destroy the loser (loser can recover if it has the Recurring ability)
  • Obligation: Loser must grant one service to gain its freedom
  • Expelled: Ejection from the Spirit World
  • Ignored: Loser might simply be ignored or skipped
  • Sundered: Limited to spirits with Deadly Ability, makes the loser into a thrall - perhaps to be exiled to the mundane world to bring fear to other mortals, or retained as a aslave in the Spirit World
