No necessary consequences, unless a GM decides one or more of the following:

Fatique Accrual

Caster can keep using magic points but gain a fatique for each extra point use.

Hit Point Attrition

Each spell or ability costs one HP from each Hit Location.

Cost is the same for any potency, magnitude or intensity.

Caster can keep casting until they receive a Major Wound.

Unconsciousness

Any character that has 0 MP falls unconscious until they regain atleast 1 MP

Willpower

Reaching zero MP, needs a Willpower roll or fall unconscious.

Can keep casting spells but each spell will make the Willpower roll at a harder difficulty than the last.

A fail makes the caster fall unconscious for 1d(number of willpower tries) days.

Endurance or Die

Make an Endurance roll and if fail then die.

If crit success then regain 1 MP.
