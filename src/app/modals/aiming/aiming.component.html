By spending an additional time aiming, a character can potentially increase their chance to hit.

Aiming requires an entire Combat Round.

Reduces the difficulty of a Situational Modifier by one grade.

Additional rounds spend aiming, grant no further advantage.

Tsinik's Notes: A cunning player will aim with their last action point so that they can shoot with their first on the next round. Fellow GMs have this in mind and act accordingly.
