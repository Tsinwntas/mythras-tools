If target at the edge, raise the difficulty 1 grade to at least Hard.

If target is thorugh a group, raise the difficulty 1 grade to at least Formidable.

If marksman fails their roll but would have succeeded if not for the aiming penalty, then they hit an adjacent victim.

GM should determine the victim randomly.

The eventual target, intended or not, if they are aware of the attack they can still Evade or Parry as normal.

Special Effects won from the attack only apply to the original target.
